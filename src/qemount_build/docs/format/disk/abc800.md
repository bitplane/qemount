# ABC 800 (Luxor floppy image)

A raw, headerless sector image for the floppy drive units of the Luxor ABC 800
series — the ABC 830/832/834/838 drives. The ABC 800 was an 8-bit professional
computer from the Swedish company Luxor (1981), the office counterpart to the
ABC 80.

## Geometry

The image carries no header; MAME selects geometry by matching the file size
against the known formats:

| Capacity | Tracks | Sides | Sectors | Bytes/sector | Encoding | Media |
|----------|--------|-------|---------|--------------|----------|-------|
| 80 KB | 40 | 1 | 16 | 128 | FM | 5.25" SD |
| 160 KB | 40 | 1 | 16 | 256 | MFM | 5.25" DD |
| 640 KB | 80 | 2 | 16 | 256 | MFM | 5.25" QD |
| 1 MB | 77 | 2 | 26 | 256 | MFM | 8" DD |
