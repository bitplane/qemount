# Bondwell 12/14 disk image

A raw, headerless MFM sector image used by MAME for the Bondwell 12 and 14
CP/M portable computers. The same loader is reused for a couple of other 5.25"
CP/M-era machines whose disks share the underlying sector layout: the
Spectravideo SVI-328 and the Kaypro II.

The file is decoded through MAME's NEC uPD765 floppy framework as a plain dump
of disk sectors; there is no container header or magic in the image, so the
variant is chosen by matching file size against the table of known geometries.

## Geometry

| System | Tracks | Sides | Sectors | Bytes/sector | Capacity | Encoding |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|--------------|----------|----------|
| Bondwell | 40 | 1 | 18 | 256 | 180 KB | MFM |
| Bondwell | 40 | 2 | 18 | 256 | 360 KB | MFM |
| SVI-328 | 40 | 1 | 17 | 256 | ~170 KB | MFM |
| SVI-328 | 40 | 2 | 17 | 256 | ~340 KB | MFM |
| Kaypro II | 40 | 1 | 10 | 512 | ~200 KB | MFM |

All variants are 5.25" double-density.
